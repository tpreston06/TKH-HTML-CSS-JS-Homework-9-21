Classic Hot Wing Receipe

Description

How to make your favorite Traditional Hot Wings in the comfort of your own home! Whether you're hosting for Game Day or just have a hankering for a hearty snack, you can't go wrong with classic hot wings. Perfectly crispy on the outside with tender juicy meat on the inside, these wings pack the perfect flavor Punch!

Ingredients | Items you'll need

  • 2lbs party wings (If buying whole wings, you will need to split them into two pieces
  • salt & pepper
  • 2 teasppons garlic powder (Optional)
  • Your favorite Hot Sauce! (I enjoy Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Hot Sauce)
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Oil (Peanut or Vegetable Oil)
  • Deep Fryer or a Dutch Oven
  • Thermometer
  • Paper Towels
  • Wire Rack
  • Medium sauce pan
  • Bleu Cheese or Ranch for Dipping!

Steps

  1. Pour oil into Deep fryer or Dutch Oven and heat to 370 degrees Fareinheit
  2. Clean and pat wings dry
  3. Add salt & pepper to taste
  4. When oil reaches desire temparature, place wings in Deep fryer or Dutch Oven a single layer so they can float freely
    **over crowding can result in longer cook times**
  5. Fry for 10 - 15 minutes or until wings are a lightly golden brown
  6. Remove from oil and place on a paper towel lined wire rack to drain
  7. In a medium sauce pan, add butter, hot sauce & garlic powder on low, until butter melts
  8. Once all wings have been cooked and dryed, toss in buffalo sauce and serve with a side of dipping sauce
